Our story

Why we started this

Most growing businesses can't justify a full in-house IT and security team, but they're exposed to exactly the same risks as larger companies — the same attackers, the same compliance pressure, the same downtime cost. We built Cyberprotek to be that team, on a budget and a timeline that actually fits an SMB.

We cover the full stack — testing, networks, servers, cameras, support, and the web — so a client never has to manage five vendors to keep one business running.
